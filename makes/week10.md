# Week 10 – Games & Play


## Artifact

This project explored the hidden labor systems behind social media and AI moderation through a visual infographic/poster.

## Reflection

This project focused on the invisible labor of content moderation. I researched how outsourced workers in countries such as Kenya and the Philippines review violent and traumatic material for major technology companies.

One case I focused on was Daniel Motaung, a former Meta content moderator in Kenya who described severe psychological trauma caused by constant exposure to graphic content.

The project revealed how technology companies externalize emotional and psychological costs onto low-paid workers while generating massive profits. This created a strong contrast between billion-dollar platforms and invisible labor systems.

I chose an infographic/poster format because it allowed me to combine statistics, emotional storytelling, and visual organization in an accessible way.

The project challenged the idea that AI systems are fully automated. Instead, it showed that large digital platforms still rely heavily on human labor that remains hidden from most users.

This project reinforced the importance of making invisible systems visible and questioning who benefits from technological systems.
