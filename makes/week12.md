# Week 12 – AI & Ecology


## Artifact

This speculative news article imagined a future where carbon-negative AI data centers become major global infrastructure systems.

## Reflection

This project explored how solving the environmental costs of AI could also increase the power and influence of computational systems.

The article imagined a future in which data centers no longer consume excessive energy, but instead generate surplus power and remove carbon from the atmosphere. While this future appears environmentally positive, it also creates new forms of political and economic dependency.

One of the central ideas in the project is that technological solutions often solve one problem while intensifying others. In this imagined future, sustainable AI infrastructure becomes deeply integrated into governance, economics, and environmental management.

The project also raised questions about ownership and inequality. Even if AI infrastructure becomes environmentally sustainable, power may still remain concentrated in corporations and wealthy nations.

This speculative artifact helped me think critically about how present-day assumptions about innovation shape imagined futures.
