### Reflection

This project explored authorship and creativity in AI-assisted systems through an interactive narrative built in Twine. The player acts as a “prompt engineer” attempting to guide an AI toward meaningful output through different prompting strategies.

The game demonstrates procedural rhetoric by making the player experience how AI output changes based on input structure. Different endings raise questions about ownership, authorship, and collaboration between humans and AI systems.

Unlike a traditional essay, the game communicates its argument through interaction and choice. This made me think differently about narrative structure and how systems themselves can shape meaning.
