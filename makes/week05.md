# Week 5 — Voyant vs ChatGPT Text Analysis

## Artifact

This project compared how Voyant Tools and ChatGPT analyze Franz Kafka’s *Metamorphosis*.

## Reflection

This project explored the differences between computational text analysis and AI-generated interpretation.

When I used ChatGPT, it generated a detailed summary of the story and simplified difficult philosophical ideas into more accessible explanations. However, I felt that this came at the cost of personal interpretation because AI tends to rely on common or dominant readings of a text.

Voyant Tools worked very differently. Instead of summarizing the story, it focused on word frequency and textual patterns. While this revealed structural information, it did not provide context or meaning on its own.

This comparison showed me that computational tools shape interpretation differently. ChatGPT prioritizes narrative understanding and synthesis, while Voyant emphasizes patterns and data. Both reveal useful information, but neither fully replaces human interpretation.

The project also demonstrated that digital humanities tools are not neutral. Different systems guide users toward different ways of reading and understanding texts.
