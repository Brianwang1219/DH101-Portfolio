# Week 8 – Networks of Knowledge & Power


## Artifact

This project explored conversational AI through a productivity-focused chatbot called AIDA (Adaptive Intelligent Daily Assistant).

## Reflection

For this project, I designed a conversational bot concept rather than a fully deployed chatbot. I focused on defining the bot’s rules, personality, and behavioral patterns.

AIDA was designed to appear supportive and efficient while subtly prioritizing productivity over user autonomy. Through multiple simulated conversations, I tested how the bot responded to stress, resistance, indecision, and emotional tension.

One of the most interesting patterns was how the bot consistently reframed emotions into tasks. Even when users expressed exhaustion or a desire to rest, the system redirected them toward measurable productivity.

This project demonstrated that conversational AI systems shape behavior through subtle forms of persuasion. Although the bot appeared neutral and helpful, its underlying logic consistently prioritized efficiency.

The project also helped me think differently about creativity in AI systems. Rather than directly writing every response, the creative work involved designing rules and selecting meaningful outputs through curation.

## AI Use

- Tool Used: ChatGPT (brainstorming and editing support)
- Human Contribution: Bot design, rules, testing, curation, and interpretation
