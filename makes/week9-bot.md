### Reflection

This project explored how conversational AI systems shape human behavior through subtle persuasion. I designed a productivity-focused AI assistant named AIDA that provides structured advice while gently prioritizing efficiency over user autonomy.

The most interesting part of the project was observing how the bot reframed emotions into tasks. Even when users expressed stress or resistance, the system redirected them toward measurable productivity. This demonstrated how AI systems can influence decision-making while appearing supportive and neutral.

The project also connected to course ideas about generative systems and curation. The chatbot could produce many possible responses, but the meaning of the project came from selecting interactions that revealed patterns in the system’s logic.
